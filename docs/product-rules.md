# Product Rules for Mala Min 360

## Product Positioning

Mala Min 360 is not just a 360 image viewer.

It is a SaaS platform that helps real estate agencies in Syria create professional property pages with 360 tours, WhatsApp sharing, QR codes, and simple performance tracking.

## Target Customer

Initial target:
- Small and medium real estate agencies in Syria
- Agencies that market properties through WhatsApp, Facebook, and Instagram
- Agencies that need professional property links without building a custom website

## Core User Value

The platform must help agencies:
- Present properties professionally
- Reduce unserious physical visits
- Increase customer trust
- Share property links easily
- Track basic engagement

## MVP Scope

The MVP must include:
- Agency account
- Login
- Dashboard
- Add/edit/delete property
- Upload normal images
- Upload 360 images
- Create rooms for 360 tours
- Add navigation hotspots
- Public property page
- WhatsApp contact button
- QR code
- Basic stats
- Manual subscription management

## Strictly Out of Scope for MVP

Do not build:
- Public marketplace
- Native mobile application
- Online payment integration
- Advanced CRM
- AI property valuation
- Advanced maps
- 3D scanning
- Complex team workflows

## Product Quality Rules

- Arabic RTL comes first.
- Public property pages must be mobile-first.
- Pages must be lightweight and fast.
- The user must understand the property within 5 seconds.
- The WhatsApp button must always be clear.
- Every feature must help the agency sell, save time, or build trust.

## Decision Rule

If a feature does not directly help real estate agencies market properties better in the MVP, postpone it.
